About me

What I love most about coding is the "Aha!" rush of joy I experience when I finally figure out how to make what I'm working on work. It's a rush I've also gotten from speaking other languages, troubleshooting electrical circuits, and working on my car. I like figuring out how stuff works, getting it to work, fixing it when it doesn't, tweaking it to suit personal taste, and finally, maintaining it in good working order. What I want most in 2021 is to "apprentice" on a team of highly-skilled programmers. I'm willing to do grunt work to get good.

Besides coding, I love: running with the Minneapolis Hash House Harriers, drumming in Circadian Ritual, & riding Minnesota singletrack trails.
