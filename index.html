About me

What I love most about coding is the "Aha!" rush of joy I experience when I finally figure out how to make what I'm working on work. It's a rush I've also gotten from speaking other languages, troubleshooting electrical circuits, and working on my car. I like figuring out how stuff works, getting it to work, fixing it when it doesn't, tweaking it to suit personal taste, and finally, maintaining it in good working order.

I can't do much, yet, but I can create a responsive, mobile-first website from scratch using HTML, CSS, & JavaScript. And I'm picking up new productivity-boosting tools like Bootstrap every week. But what I want most in 2021 is to join a team of skilled programmers who craft high-quality software that satisfies their clients' needs and produces tranquility.
